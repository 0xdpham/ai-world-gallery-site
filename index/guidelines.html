Community Guidelines

Rules and standards that keep AIWorldGallery.com a safe, creative, and trustworthy space for everyone.

These guidelines are binding on all users. Violations may result in content removal, suspension, or permanent ban.

Core Principles

Our platform is built on five pillars: AI-Created Content Only, Respect for all creators and rights holders, No illegal or harmful content, Transparency about AI tool usage, and Compliance with applicable laws.

What You May Upload

You may upload original AI-generated images, videos (maximum 3 minutes), music, audio, and AI-assisted creative works — provided you hold the rights to share them and the content complies with these guidelines.

Strictly Prohibited Content

Rule 1 — No Adult Content: Pornography, sexually graphic or explicit material, sexual content intended for arousal, fetish content, and explicit nudity in a sexualized context are all strictly prohibited. Zero tolerance.

Rule 2 — No Self-Harm Content: Content that promotes, depicts, glorifies, or instructs on suicide or self-harm in any form is prohibited.

Rule 3 — No Content Promoting Violence Against Others: Content that promotes, depicts, or glorifies murder, assault, rape, or any other form of violence against individuals is prohibited.

Rule 4 — No Illicit Drug Content: Content promoting, depicting, or glorifying the use, manufacture, or distribution of illegal substances is prohibited.

Rule 5 — No Political Violence: Content promoting, inciting, or glorifying political violence, terrorism, or extremist activity is strictly prohibited.

Rule 6 — No External Links: Links to outside websites, platforms, or services are not permitted in submissions, profiles, or comments.

Rule 7 — No Promotion of Competing Platforms: Advertising, promoting, or redirecting users to competing platforms is prohibited.

Additional Prohibited Content: Content infringing copyrights or trademarks; deceptive deepfakes of real individuals; hate speech; CSAM (zero tolerance, reported to authorities); and misrepresentation of AI tool usage or authorship.

Your Responsibilities

When uploading, you confirm the content is AI-generated, you have the right to share it, you have disclosed the AI tools used, and the content complies with all rules. You are solely responsible for your submissions.

Enforcement

We use automated systems, human review, and community reports. Violations may result in content removal, visibility restrictions, warnings, account suspension, or permanent ban. Appeals must be submitted within the stated timeframe and are reviewed by a different moderator when possible.

Last updated: February 2026. These guidelines may be updated as the platform evolves.
