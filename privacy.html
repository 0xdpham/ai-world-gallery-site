Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your full name, email address, phone number, and chosen username. We also collect content you submit, tool disclosures, and standard usage data (IP address, browser type, pages visited).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: operate and improve the platform; verify your identity and content attestations; communicate with you about your account; enforce our Community Guidelines and Terms of Use; and comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with: service providers who assist us in operating the platform (under strict confidentiality agreements); law enforcement when required by law or to protect our users; and in connection with a business transfer or acquisition.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. Note that deletion of your account will remove your profile and submissions from the platform.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain login sessions and analyze site usage. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function correctly.
